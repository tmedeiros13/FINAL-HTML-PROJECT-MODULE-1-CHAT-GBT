Filmmaker Profile: Shannon Chance

Shannon Chance is an emerging independent filmmaker based in Dallas, Texas. A Bridge City native, Shannon's passion for storytelling was ignited during his time at the University of Texas, where he majored in Film and Media Studies. His debut short film, Ten West, premiered at the inaugural Texas Film Belt showcase in September 2025, earning accolades for its authentic portrayal of Southeast Texas life.

A short film depicting the intersecting lives of travelers at a Texas crossroads, delving into human connections.
